Arch Linux help

Since we are officially listed, you may just uncomment our mirror in /etc/pacman.d/mirrorlist.

In other cases, you may add these lines to your /etc/pacman.d/mirrorlist:


Server = https://pkg.adfinis-sygroup.ch/archlinux/$repo/os/$arch
Server = http://pkg.adfinis-sygroup.ch/archlinux/$repo/os/$arch

Additional information

You can find further information about our mirror on the following site:

  • https://www.archlinux.org/mirrors/adfinis-sygroup.ch/

CentOS help

Since we are officially listed, you don't need to configure anything special. Yum gets a computed list from http://mirrorlist.centos.org/?release=$releasever&arch=$basearch&repo=os.

If you wish, you can change the baseurl in /etc/yum.repos.d/CentOS-Base.repo:


baseurl=http://pkg.adfinis-sygroup.ch/centos/$releasever/os/$basearch

Additional information

You can find further information about our mirror on the following site:

  • http://mirror-status.centos.org/#ch

Debian help

Add the following lines to your /etc/apt/sources.list:

Replace "jessie" with your release.


deb http://pkg.adfinis-sygroup.ch/debian/ jessie main non-free contrib
deb-src http://pkg.adfinis-sygroup.ch/debian/ jessie main non-free contrib

deb http://security.debian.org/ jessie/updates main
deb-src http://security.debian.org/ jessie/updates main

deb http://pkg.adfinis-sygroup.ch/debian/ jessie-updates main contrib non-free
deb-src http://pkg.adfinis-sygroup.ch/debian/ jessie-updates main contrib non-free

Additional information

You can find further information about our mirror on the following site:

  • http://mirror.debian.org/status.html

Replace "jessie" with your release.

EPEL help

Since we are officially listed, you can just install the corresponding epel-release package on your system:


yum install http://pkg.adfinis-sygroup.ch/epel/epel-release-latest-5.noarch.rpm
yum install http://pkg.adfinis-sygroup.ch/epel/epel-release-latest-6.noarch.rpm
yum install http://pkg.adfinis-sygroup.ch/epel/epel-release-latest-7.noarch.rpm

Yum will then get a computed list from https://mirrors.fedoraproject.org/metalink?repo=epel-7&arch=$basearch.

Additional information

You can find further information about our mirror on the following site:

  • https://admin.fedoraproject.org/mirrormanager/mirrors

nodejs help

NodeJS 7.x is mirrored for the following Distributions:
Ubuntu: sid, trusty, xenial, yakkety and zesty
Debian: jessie

Add the following lines to your /etc/apt/sources.list:

Replace "jessie" with your corresponding release.


deb http://pkg.adfinis-sygroup.ch/nodejs/node_7.x jessie main

Ubuntu help

Add the following lines to your /etc/apt/sources.list:

Replace "vivid" with your release.


deb http://pkg.adfinis-sygroup.ch/ubuntu/ vivid main restricted universe multiverse
# deb-src http://pkg.adfinis-sygroup.ch/ubuntu/ vivid main restricted universe multiverse

deb http://pkg.adfinis-sygroup.ch/ubuntu/ vivid-updates main restricted universe multiverse
# deb-src http://pkg.adfinis-sygroup.ch/ubuntu/ vivid-updates main restricted universe multiverse


deb http://pkg.adfinis-sygroup.ch/ubuntu/ vivid-backports main restricted universe multiverse
